Our Story

Independent. Modern.
Distinctly Norwich.

Crown Hotel is a new independent hotel situated on the western edge of Norwich, conveniently close to the Norfolk and Norwich University Hospital and the Norwich Research Park — making it an ideal base for medical professionals, academics, and business travellers alike.

With 32 well-appointed rooms across four categories, a gym, restaurant, bar, and dedicated meeting spaces, we offer everything you need for a comfortable and productive stay — without compromise.
